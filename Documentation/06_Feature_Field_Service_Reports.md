# Feature: Field Service Reports

> **Surfaces:** Tier A only (iOS, Android, Mac, Windows).

## 1. Smart Reporting Profiles
The app automatically adjusts the reporting interface based on the publisher's specific privileges.
- **Regular Publishers:** The UI simplifies the process, only asking: *"Did you share in the ministry this month? (Yes/No)"* and *"How many Bible Studies did you conduct?"*
- **Pioneers & Missionaries:** The UI automatically expands to allow the input of total hours, in addition to the standard fields.

## 2. The Escalation Ladder (Reminders)
To relieve the Secretary from the massive burden of chasing down late reports, the app handles reminders using an automated escalation ladder that appropriately shifts responsibility to the Field Service Group Overseers.

- **Day 1:** A warm, automated push notification is sent to all publishers: *"Happy new month! Please tap here to submit your report."*
- **Day 4:** A second automated nudge is sent directly to any publisher who has not yet submitted.
- **Day 5 (Group Overseer Alert):** The responsibility shifts. The app sends a local notification to the Group Overseers, listing the specific publishers in their group who are still outstanding (e.g., Publisher A, B, and C). The UI provides a "1-Click WhatsApp" button next to each name, pre-filling a friendly reminder text.
- **Day 8:** A final reminder is sent to the Group Overseers for any remaining stragglers in their group.
- **Day 10 (Secretary Escalation):** The app notifies the Congregation Secretary, providing a list of which Group Overseers they need to follow up with.

## 3. Data Distribution & Backups
While a publisher's report is highly sensitive, it is completely visible to all Elders for shepherding purposes. 
- When a publisher hits "Submit," their app generates a lockbox addressed to **every Elder in the congregation**. 
- This ensures all Elders have the context they need to shepherd their groups effectively. Crucially, it also provides multiple decentralized backups of the congregation's most vital data in case the Secretary loses or damages their device.

## 4. The Shepherding Dashboard
Every Elder has access to a Field Service Dashboard to monitor the spiritual health of the congregation.
- **Congregation Health:** Instantly view active publishers, outstanding reports, and overall congregation statistics for the month.
- **Pioneer Tracking:** A dedicated section tracks every Pioneer's progress towards their annual hour requirement. 
- **Automated Flags:** The dashboard automatically flags any Pioneer who falls **50 hours behind** their required schedule. This 50-hour threshold signifies they are essentially a full month behind and immediately alerts the Elders to step in and offer shepherding, encouragement, or assistance before the deficit becomes unmanageable.
